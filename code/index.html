Welcome to Dufferin Rutherford Veterinary Hospital

Dufferin / Rutherford Veterinary Hospital is dedicated to maintaining the health of your pet and emphasizes constant monitoring of your pets health status. The latest technology and procedures to assure your pet is kept healthy and active for years to come. However, what really makes your animal friend happy at our hospital is more than just our services. Its the way we treat your pet as though it were our very own.
We take the extra time to learn and remember your pets name, get to know its likes and dislikes, and learn what makes it special. And you can be happy, too, because we care about more than your pet. We also care about you and make the extra effort to personally phone you following any surgical procedure or medical treatment to check on you and your pet. Its also why we always send your pet home in tip-top shape. We provide the best health care service for your pet from head to tail because we believe this is the best way to show your pet how much we care.
